thermostat: The thermostat operates the heating in the hallway. Just adjust to the temperature you want and the radiators will come on and cut out automatically when that temperature is reached. You can adjust the radiators individually as well. 

hotwater: For the hot water, the timer is set to come on 4 times throughout the day. We have never struggled for hot water with 6 people using the house but if you have any issues please call us. Please do not touch the programmer in the utility room. Please also beware that the boiler chimney/flue gets very hot and will melt anything sitting against it with the potential to cause a fire. 

logburner: There is a log-burning stove in the living room that you are welcome to use. Firelighters, kindling and logs are provided in the basket. You can buy more logs at the Auchentulloch Farm Shop or most petrol stations if needed. 

warning1: Please do not leave the property with the log burner lit. 

warning2: The fire guard must always be in place when the fire is on. 

warning3: Please never dispose of ashes in the bin or outside until at least 24 hours after the fire has fully gone out. We take care of ash disposal during cleaning.
